All Natural

Our candles are crafted from the finest natural resources. We use materials such as beeswax, soy, and coconut, combined with pure oils for a clean, long-lasting burn.

Eco friendly

Our candles are made from the natural waxes mentioned above. They are exceptionally eco-friendly. Beeswax is a bi-product of honey production, soy wax comes from renewable soybean crops, and coconut wax is derived from sustainably harvested meat of coconuts.
